[Lex18](https://archiveofourown.org/users/Lex18/pseuds/Lex18) inspired my depiction of the George / Leon ship in [Ironing Out The Kinks](https://archiveofourown.org/works/48758389). I did not think of the pairing on my own, but the two make a lot of sense together. I really like seeing them as a functional narrative foil to the disaster that is Arthur and Merlin. I know I did not exactly depict a smut scene, but know that there is more to come. I didn't want to distract from the current focus.

I also thought it was endearing for Freya to just not particularly care who Leon is, becasue he isn&apos;t part of her responsiblities as a logistics-type servant. I just like giving the characters who work in roles that are "invisible", especially in the show, some screen time. This might be because of my day-job is done well when nobody knows it is happening at all.
