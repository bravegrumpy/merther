#### Morgana

A shout of “My Cabbagaes” coming from the market, visible from the window, startled Morgana away from her letter. While she couldn’t _stand_ what Arthur did in his spare time, she clearly did not have enough political support to overthrow him.

She idly thought about her child, and wondered what the druids named him. He would be about twelve by now. She wished that she could have at least met him, but it was better this way.

From her vantage point, Morgana could see chaos, and some vegetable stands sullied by what looked like either bird droppings or worse, seed.
