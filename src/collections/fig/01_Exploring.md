As the sun set, mosquitos hummed, aiming to snag some juicy red and green vitality from wanderers outside.  Ever since the most recent iteration of remodeling the Bridge Across started again, the standing pools of murky brown standing water, attracted the pests. Fig intentionally did not attempt to itch the budding bruise just beneath his work pants. He had another mile to walk as he just missed the bus to get himself home.

Today was pointless, just like any other day. Despite unsuccessfully looking for work, Fig was at least gratful for the DiaMont foundation, for helping him find housing. 

Zipping up his 'trap, to ensure no accidents occurred, Fig inserted one headphone into his left ear, and tuned his broken BrightLight to a punk metal band, headed up by a Siklish Singer, relocated into the center of TriVerment City.  He could feel the swell of his fingers, up against the now-sweaty metal of the rings around his fingers. The flashy lights, hum of workers started to cause a bit of a migraine. But the music, and the too-quickly fading effects of the cheese and blue-drink can made it tolerable. 

His BrightLight buzzed, momentarily interrupting the lyrics comparing "cyan" and "suicide", paired with a guitar lick he wished he could replicate.

"Wanna play online?" the notification flicked on his screen. He ignored the text for now, looking around for a more compelling way to find quiet. 

After passing through a darkened alley, full of expensive shops and restaurants, that bustled during the day, Fig made a right turn.  Sitting on a stoop, in front of an empty lot, with an unintelligable sign at the entrance, was a fat man, tapping away with one foot, seemingly zoned out.
