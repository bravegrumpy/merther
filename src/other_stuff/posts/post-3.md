Writing is hard.  Everybody knows this. That is why people spend so much time trying to outsource the writing bits to a computer.  Some people have the gift, but it is a challenge for me. 

I have found that with chapter 6, I am at a natural "stopping point" for the chapter, but it hasn't reached my made-up soft minimum of 3K words. I don't want it to get all rambly.  That being said, the chapter focuses on a side OC that is not part of the main cast. 

I need to figure out where the story is going next.  The first five chapters were one chunk, it showed Merlin getting assaulted, then it showed my main cast working together.  Chapter 6 is an interlude into one of Merlin's victims.

I also realize that I need the day to roll over.

The interlude might be the right way to let the day roll over, while adding comic relief.
