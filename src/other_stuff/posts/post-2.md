There are a lot of neocities sites that seem to have this one layout. I mean I guess it is a closer to one of about a dozen layouts that seem to be going around. I have found that I'm typically drawn to sites that don't use one of these pre-made layouts.  

One of the "bigger" layout sources is [Teppy's Layouts](https://teppyslayouts.neocities.org/). This particularl source suggests the use of iframes to create layouts.  This is an approach I would hever have thought of myself.  It basically has "content" exist as iframes within a "main" page.  I will say that these layouts are innovative. Furthermore, the creative-customize-it culture of Neocities (and Nekoweb) sites tend to allow for a ton of customization.  But ultimately, the point of using a layout, is to *use* the layout-y parts of the layout.
