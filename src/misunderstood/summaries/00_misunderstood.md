Years ago, Arthur brutally assaulted and raped Merlin. When the constant sexual abuse finally seemed to wane—just long enough for Merlin to lower his guard, Arthur strikes again. This AU follows Merlin and his friends as they navigate the perilous game of deciding who to trust while coping and resisting a merciless tyrant. Are Merlin and Arthur still two sides of the same coin? Who will win Camelon's coin toss of destiny?
