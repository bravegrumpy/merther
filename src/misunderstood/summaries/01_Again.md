Years ago, Arthur brutally assaulted raped Merlin. While most of the sexual abuse has died down, Arthur is still abusive.

Despite this, Merlin keeps his position as servant to the King, as he understands that he holds the true power of Camelot. One day Merlin is careless, and allows himself to relax a little bit, while dissociating. Arthur, sensing calm, flirts with Merlin, triggering the worst trauma response that Merlin has had in years. Merlin uses magic to get himself out of the situation.
