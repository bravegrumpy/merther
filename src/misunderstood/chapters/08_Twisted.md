#### Gwenivere

At long last, the door to the armory door opened.  The tall, blonde man, styled as a king walked in. Gwen gripped one of her daggers more tightly than was strictly necessary, in order to steady herself.  She hoped that she did not betray any weaknesses.

*Take a deep breath. Don't betray the degree of your nerves,* Gwen thought to herself.  She straightened her shoulders, hoping to hide her superior chain mail from view.

"I said, what do you have for *me*," Arthur said, licking his lips. The armory was not well ventilated.  A pool of sweat dripped from Arthur's upper lip.  He might have been handsome if he weren't so cruel.

Gwen simultaneously drew her ordinary sword, and her dagger, moving the sword forward, angling some etching for Arthur. 

"I can etch each knight's name on the blade like so," she said, in a measured tone, displaying the etching of her own name on the flat of the blade.  She had considered writing Lancelot's name, but decided against revealing more than she needed of herself. 

Arthur swatted the blade away, with his gloved hand, "Youch!"

Gwen tried not to giggle, "It is sharp. Did you not know that this is a *sword*"

"How dare you raise your weapon at royalty?" Arthur raised one eyebrow.  

"I was *trying* to show the sword you requested," Gwen responded.

She did not use any honorifics.
